Our Property Management team have decades of experience and expertise which they apply to the management of every rental property. We have experience in both residential and commercial property management of all sizes and scales – from single apartments, to multi-apartment buildings, from commercial towers to retail strips and warehouses. No property is too large or small to benefit from our professionalism and expertise.

Marketing and Tenant Selection : We arrange all aspects of marketing your rental property including arranging and managing viewing of the property by prospective tenants. Our team will screen applications for lease of your property and select the appropriate tenants.

Contracts and Legal Documentation : Our Property Management team will organise and manage all legal aspects of the lease agreement with the tenant.

Rent Collection : We have an excellent rent collection process in place which ensures that rent is paid in a timely manner, and funds are forwarded to owners without delay.

Property Maintenance : We undertake preventative maintenance in all our managed properties. This initiative allows us to fix many problems at an early stage, reducing overall costs. We are also able to attend to emergency maintenance issues should these occur.

Dispute Resolution : If a dispute with a tenant arises, we are fully conversant with legislation regarding the rights of both owners and tenants and able to provide support and advice.

What do we do?

Find Tenants
Our professional team will screen prospective tenants to ensure your property is leased to the right person.
Manage Your Property
We manage all administrative tasks relating to your property rental and maintain communication with both you, the owner, and your tenants.
Maintain Your Property
We have a team of contractors who we can call on to undertake any maintenance work on your property. Our team of contractors offer competitive prices, and copies of invoices are provided to the owner on completion of work. You can also choose to allocate work to your own preferred contactors.
Legal Matters
Legislation regarding tenants and landlords changes frequently. Our role includes keeping in touch with these changes and how they affect you and your tenants. In the case of a default by the tenant, we handle any bounced cheques and will take care of eviction proceedings according to current legislation.
